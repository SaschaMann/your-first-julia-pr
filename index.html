Update

To reduce spam, maintainers need to opt-in to Hacktoberfest now. If you submit a PR to fix an issue labelled with 'hacktoberfest' and the repository does not have the 'hacktoberfest' topic, you can kindly ask the maintainer to label your PR as 'hacktoberfest-accepted' for it to count towards your Hacktoberfest goal.
